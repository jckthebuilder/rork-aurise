# Wayv — Premium Wake-Up System Onboarding Flow

## Overview

Build a complete, production-quality 19-screen onboarding flow for **Wayv** — a task-based wake-up system for people who struggle to get out of bed. Light theme, warm sunrise accents, premium typography, emotionally intelligent copy.

---

## Features

- **19-screen guided onboarding** that builds a personalized wake-up plan
- **Emotional storytelling arc** — identity opener → self-diagnosis → plan building → premium offer → completion
- **Single-select question cards** with elegant tap interactions and haptic feedback
- **Time picker screens** for target and current wake-up times
- **Mission selection** with free options (Math, Photo) and locked premium previews
- **Dynamic mission explanation** that adapts to the user's choice
- **Alarm sound selection** with play/preview controls (visual only for now)
- **Weekday picker** with pill-style day toggles
- **Plan summary card** showing all personalized choices
- **Premium paywall** — strong but fair, with a clear free continuation path
- **Notification permission** request with warm-up context
- **Completion screen** with final plan summary and "Open my app" action
- **Smooth progress indicator** throughout the entire flow
- **Spring animations** and transitions between screens

---

## Design

- **Theme**: Light background with warm sunrise accent colors (soft amber/coral tones)
- **Typography**: SF Pro with bold/heavy weights for headlines, creating strong visual hierarchy
- **Spacing**: Generous whitespace, 8pt grid, large touch targets
- **Cards**: Subtle rounded cards with light borders for selection options
- **Progress bar**: Thin, warm-colored bar at the top showing flow progress
- **Animations**: Smooth spring transitions between screens, subtle fade-ins for content
- **Haptics**: Selection feedback on card taps, success feedback on key moments
- **Accent color**: Warm amber/sunrise orange for CTAs and progress — not system blue
- **Overall feel**: Calm, premium, confident — like a high-end wellness app

---

## Screens

1. **Identity Opener** — "Become someone who gets out of bed." with subtle sunrise icon, "Build my plan" CTA
2. **Product Thesis** — "Stop snoozing. Start actually waking up." with trust line
3. **Morning Self-Perception** — "Do mornings come naturally to you?" with 3 elegant selection cards
4. **Main Pain Point** — "What usually keeps you in bed?" with 5 options
5. **Current Alarm Behavior** — "How many alarms do you usually set?" with 4 options
6. **Reliability Check** — "If you only set one alarm, would that actually work?" with 3 options
7. **Emotional Reframe** — "This isn't laziness. It's a pattern." — compassionate trust-building
8. **Target Wake-Up Time** — Elegant time picker for desired wake-up
9. **Current Wake-Up Time** — Same picker for actual current wake-up
10. **Motivation Interstitial** — "If you win the morning, you win the day."
11. **Mission Selection** — Choose Math or Photo (free), see locked premium missions
12. **Mission Explanation** — Dynamic screen explaining why the chosen mission works
13. **Alarm Sound Selection** — List of 6 sounds with play controls
14. **Alarm Intensity** — Gentle / Standard / Hard to ignore cards
15. **Active Days** — Weekday pill toggles, weekdays pre-selected
16. **Plan Summary** — Beautiful summary card of the complete wake-up plan
17. **Premium Paywall** — "Unlock the full wake-up system" with fair free path
18. **Notification Permission** — "Make sure your alarm can reach you" with system prompt
19. **Completion** — "You're set for tomorrow morning" with final summary
